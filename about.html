ABOUT ME

I am a Grade 11 student at Henry Wise Wood High School.
I have various interests, including sports, gaming,
programming, and spending time with my loved ones.
In terms of sports, I play tier 1 for Foothills FC and serve
as the team's starting left-back. I also play on the Henry
Wise Wood soccer team, where I was selected Co-captain
and played central-defensive-midfielder, leading the team
to the Calgary High School Sports Association playoff finals.
I also enjoy programming, which is why I decided to pursue
this project: to improve my skill set and work with something
I'm passionate about. When I have spare time, I like to spend it
my friends and family because they help me escape from the
demands of academics and regroup. I also like spending
quality time with my dog Coco, a 5-year-old Bichon Shih Tzu.
Finally, I like playing video games on my console. I frequently
play multiplayer games with my friends, and time flies when I do!
