MY HOBBIES

As mentioned previously, I love to play soccer. I used to
be a part of a great club team but unfortunately stopped
playing club soccer to focus on school. Although, I am the co
captain of my school's soccer team and was the top scorer.
One of my favorite memories playing soccer was a
tournament I attended with my club team. It was in Vancouver
and was a great team-bonding experience as everyone got
closer with one another. We won gold that tournament and it was
definitely one of the best weekends of my life! Whenever I have
free time, I spend time playing on my
console with my friend and family. With my family, we
play games that are locally multiplayer such as simulator
sports game where we have fun competing against eachother.
With my friends, we play games that are multiplayer such as
pvp or sports games. We play games such as Rocket League,
Fortnite, and Fifa. Through gaming, I build stronger bonds
with my loved ones.
